3. 🌪️ Clima & Catástrofes

Jamaica secures $200m of parametric hurricane insurance with third catastrophe bond

The Government of Jamaica has now successfully secured its one-third upsized target of $200 million of parametric named storm and hurricane insurance protection from its new World Bank facilitated IBRD CAR Jamaica 2026 catastrophe bond issuance, pricing the coverage within guidance, while ILS funds were the main supporters of this deal. As we reported, Jamaica came back to market for its third catastrophe bond sponsorship at the end of April, initially targeting $150 million of parametric named storm and hurricane protection . Jamaica first secured parametric hurricane insurance to add to its disaster risk protection with the support of the capital markets through the $185 million IBRD CAR 130 cat bond in 2021.
