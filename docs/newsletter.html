Lockton Re, the reinsurance arm of the independent broking group, has announced the full deployment of its advisory and structuring capabilities dedicated to Brazil’s Letra de Risco de Seguro (LRS /Insurance Risk Letters) market, utilising locally licensed Special Purpose Securitisation Entities (SSPE).
